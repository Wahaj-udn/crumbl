Our Story

Baked with Love Since 2025

Crumble & Co. started in a small kitchen with a big dream. Our founder, Harisa, had a passion for creating the perfect cookie - one that was crispy on the outside yet soft and chewy on the inside.

What began as baking for friends and family quickly turned into a local sensation, and before long, we opened our first store. Today, we ship our handcrafted cookies nationwide while maintaining the same quality and love in every batch.

We use only the finest ingredients - premium Belgian chocolate, farm-fresh eggs, real butter, and our secret family recipes that have been perfected over generations.
